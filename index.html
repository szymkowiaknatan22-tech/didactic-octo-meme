Controls

Movement

W/A/S/D - Move character

Input Modes (Switch Anytime)

F1 - Click-to-Fire
Move cursor with touchpad/mouse, click to shoot
F2 - Auto-Fire
Move cursor, automatically fires at cursor position
F3 - Keyboard-Only
Use I/J/K/L to aim, Space to shoot

Menu Controls

ESC - Pause/Menu
Shift - Precision aim (slower cursor)

How to Play

  1. Choose your input mode (F1, F2, or F3) based on your preference
  2. Start in a safe room and explore the floor
  3. Clear combat rooms by defeating all enemies
  4. Doors unlock when rooms are cleared
  5. Choose artifact rewards after clearing rooms to power up
  6. Find and defeat the boss to complete the floor
  7. Adjust settings (ESC ‚Üí Settings) to fine-tune touchpad controls
