## Color Scheme
- Background: Dark gray/black (15, 15, 20)
- Walls: Medium gray (60, 60, 70)
- Floor: Dark gray (25, 25, 30)
- Player: Cyan/Blue (100, 200, 255)
- Enemies: Red/Orange/Purple variants
- Projectiles: Yellow (player) / Red (enemy)
- UI: Light gray text (220, 220, 220)
- HP Bar: Green (100, 255, 100)
- Crosshair: Yellow (255, 255, 0)
